# Contributing to Project Kintsugi

Thank you for your interest in improving the SaaS Japan Expansion Playbook! We welcome contributions from Product Managers, Strategists, and Localization Experts.

### How to Contribute
1. **Report a Gap:** Open an "Issue" if you find a market risk or a cultural nuance we missed.
2. **Submit a Case Study:** If you have data on Japanese SaaS trends, please submit a Pull Request.
3. **Refine the Narrative:** Help us sharpen the 6-page memo for clarity and impact.

### Contribution Standards
* **Tone:** Professional, objective, and customer-centric.
* **Format:** Follow the Amazon narrative style (no bullet points in the core memo).
* **Data:** Please provide sources or "simulated data logic" for any financial claims.
